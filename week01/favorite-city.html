Arequipa

Arequipa is a city is one of the most important cities in Peru. It is known as “The White City” because many of its buildings are made from a white volcanic stone called sillar. Arequipa has beautiful colonial architecture, historic churches, and a famous main square called the Plaza de Armas of Arequipa. The city is surrounded by impressive volcanoes such as Misti Volcano. Arequipa is also known for its rich culture, traditional food, and important historical sites like the Santa Catalina Monastery.
