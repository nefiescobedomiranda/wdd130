Peru Rapids Rafting

At Peru Rapids Rafting, our whitewater rafting trips are designed to give adventurers an unforgettable experience on the wild rivers of Peru. Our professional guides focus on safety, teamwork, and fun while you navigate exciting rapids and enjoy the beauty of nature. Paddle together, feel the adrenaline, and discover the true spirit of adventure.

HISTORY

Peru Rapids Rafting was founded in 2000 by a group of adventure enthusiasts who shared a passion for rivers and outdoor exploration.

Over the years, the company expanded its expeditions and continues to provide safe and exciting rafting experiences on the rivers of Peru.
